How to Join & Trade Safely
Simple rules to keep the community clean and your money safe.
Read this before you buy or sell any account.
  • 1
    Use a real Facebook account
    No fresh empty ids, fake photos or new accounts made only for trading. We only trade with real profiles.
  • 2
    Middleman through admins only
    Never send money directly to a random buyer/seller. Always use an official admin as middleman for payments and account transfer.
  • 3
    Full proof of account
    Ask for in-game screenshots, link status, change email options and any previous owner info before confirming the deal.
  • 4
    Record your trade
    Keep chat, screenshots and screen-record if possible. This helps our support team solve any dispute quickly.
  • 5
    No blacklisted users
    Anyone reported for scam, abuse or chargeback will be banned from the community and publicly warned.

FAQ
Quick answers to common questions new members usually ask.
Can I trade without middleman to save fee?
Technically you can, but we strongly do not recommend it. Without middleman protection, we can't help if the other side scams or blocks you.
What happens if someone tries to scam?
Collect proof (screenshots / video), contact our dispute admin, and send all info. Confirmed scammers are banned & added to blacklist.
Do you give refund if account is recovered later?
Each case is checked manually. Recovery after trade is investigated with full proof from both sides. If seller is guilty, they are banned and we try our best to compensate buyer where possible.
Can I promote my own trading page / shop?
Yes, but only with permission. Contact an admin for partnership / sponsor options. Unapproved promotion or spam will be removed.
